üìñ About MindPath

MindPath is a mobile-first mental health wellness application designed specifically for university students navigating academic stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges.

This prototype demonstrates how AI-powered tools can provide accessible, stigma-free mental health support through validated clinical assessments (GAD-7), voice-based emotional analysis, and personalized wellness recommendations.

Built with a calming aesthetic featuring soft pinks, sage tones, and dusty blues, MindPath creates a safe, welcoming space for self-reflection and mental wellness tracking.

Project Highlights

  • Clinical GAD-7 anxiety assessment integration
  • AI-powered voice memo analysis
  • Real-time stress level tracking
  • Interactive data visualization
  • Mobile-optimized single-screen design
  • No scrolling required - all content fits perfectly
  • Responsive and accessible interface
